TI CYCLES

TI Cycles, part of the Murugappa Group, was established in 1949 and is one of India's leading bicycle manufacturers. Known for brands like Hercules, BSA, Montra, and Mach City, it introduced India’s first MTB, geared bike, and cycling café. The company operates manufacturing plants in Chennai, Nasik, and Noida, and has a strong dealer network with over 1,200 outlets. It also runs Track & Trail stores for bicycles, accessories, and fitness equipment, promoting cycling as a lifestyle.TI Cycles has been at the forefront of cycling innovations in India, such as introducing the first mountain bike (MTB), geared bikes, and India’s first carbon fiber frame bicycle.
